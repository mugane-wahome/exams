1. a. Martin Luther King Jr.

6. a. Winston Churchill

7. a. Nelson Mandela

8. a. Ronald Reagan

   - The speech titled "A Time for Choosing" was delivered by Ronald Reagan in 1964 during his support for Barry Goldwater's presidential campaign.
